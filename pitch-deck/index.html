The Problem

A healthcare collapse in disconnected zones.

The Cloud Illusion

J&K experiences the highest frequency of internet blackouts globally. When a crisis hits, "cloud-based" health apps simply die.

The ASHA Burden

Specialists avoid rural transfers. ASHA workers are left alone during winter to make life-or-death triage decisions without guidance.

Mental Health Stigma

Over 36% of young adults face severe psychological distress, but there are only 41 psychiatrists. Stigma prevents seeking help.

The Solution

The Dual Offline Ecosystem

A 100% offline, voice-first Clinical Copilot natively speaking Koshur, Urdu, Gojri, and Pahari.

Tier 1: The Offline APK

  • Target: Frontline ASHA Workers
  • Platform: Android App on government tablets
  • Hardware Cost: ₹0. Highly scalable.
  • Impact: Instant triage protocols during winter emergencies, drastically reducing cognitive load.

Tier 2: The Rugged Device

  • Target: Extreme Off-Grid Scenarios
  • Hardware: Solar-compatible Raspberry Pi box
  • Deployment: Nomadic Gujjar/Bakarwal tents, LoC border bunkers.
  • Impact: Communal, zero-hour first responder.
