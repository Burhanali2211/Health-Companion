Technical Differentiation

Why this works
where everything else didn't.

Four decisions made at the foundation — not bolted on later.

Offline-first, not offline-mode

Internet was never part of the design — not a fallback. Whisper STT, the rule engine, and Coqui TTS all live on a 32 GB microSD. The device has no cloud account and never needed one.

Language-native, not translated

Urdu and Koshur built in from line 1 of code — not translated from an English base. OpenAI Whisper was trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual speech. It natively handles Kashmiri-accented Urdu without modification.

Season-aware, not weather-aware

8 named Kashmiri seasons with distinct health logic — not generic summer/winter. Chilla Kalan triggers Kangri CO poisoning alerts and hypothermia rules. Chilla Bachha triggers different ones. That distinction is the difference between useful advice and dangerous advice.

Field hardware, not a phone app

A Raspberry Pi 5 with a 7" touch display, USB mic, speaker, and 6-hour battery pack. Total cost: ~₹7,000. Can be deployed in an Anganwadi center, a rural clinic, or a mountain village. No smartphone required. No data plan. No monthly subscription.
